The Royale Senate - Hebbal

The Royale Senate flaunts an astute blend of natural splendor and modern architecture with high-tech amenities. This luxury hotel will serve as a perfect place for both, business and leisure travelers. It is conveniently located in Bangalore City near HEBBAL FLYOVER enroute to International Airport. The hotel elevator leads to 30 beautifully furnished guest rooms and suites, a state of the art conference hall and a fine dining experience at "Saaga".

Distance from Bus/Railway Station and Airport

  • BANGALORE / MAJESTIC BUS STATION - 9 KMS
  • BANGALORE CITY RAILWAY STATION - 9.5 KMS
  • KEMPEGOWDA INTERNATIONAL AIRPORT - 29 KMS

Near by attraction include:


Lumbini Gardens (1 Km)

Families staying at the Country Inn & Suites can find hours of fun at this nearby amusement park that features children's rides, water attractions and scenic views of Nagavara Lake.


ISKCON Temple (11.5 km)

A must-see for any Bengaluru visit, this majestic temple boasts a stunning construction of glass and gopuram, and it serves as a religious and cultural centre for the surrounding region.


Bangalore Palace (6 Km)

Originally built to reflect the same Tudor-style architecture as Windsor Castle, this imposing edifice now houses an impressive art collection within its walls and an amusement park just outside the palace.
