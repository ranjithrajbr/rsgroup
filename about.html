Our Story

Food is symbolic of love when words are inadequate.

It is popularly said that food is the ingredient that binds people together. We consider our staff and our customers as a close knit extended family. We have gained reputation for food that speaks authenticity, taste, exceptional quality, great hospitality and bringing a global cuisine to the city of Bangalore. Our most iconic establishment, RS Shiv Sagar is symbolic to Bangalore and is a popular landmark.

Vision

The RS group aims to elevate its position as an industry leader, focussing on quality, taste, hygiene and diversity.

Misson

  • Enhancing our process and practices to provide our patrons with nothing but the best.

  • Improving the quality of life for our employees and providing opportunities for growth.

  • Ensuring the well being of our partners, patrons and well wishers.
